My Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is building plastic model kits. However, things like model planes, cars, or trains don't particularly interest me. What I build are known as Gundam plastic models. "Gunpla" for short. Gunpla is extremely popular, it's been around for the past three decades or so and has attracted the attention of model builders of all skill levels from all over the world. So much so that there are yearly building championships which give people the chance to show off their modelling prowess to a worldwide audience. I don't go that far, building the kits is enough for me.

Pictured above is the Perfect Grade RX-0 Unit 3 "Phenex." Perfect Grade kits are the pinnacle of Gunpla kits, they are the second largest kits you can get featuring the most parts, most intricate builds, and special gimmicks. For example, there is an add-on you can get for the kit which lights up the internal skeleton a bright blue color. Beautiful for sure, but also extremely expensive. Without the lighting kit, the Phenex alone costs over $700. It's for that reason most builders stick with high grades and master grades which can range from $20 - $80. I can't get this kit yet, but one day I will. Interested in learning more about the history of the hobby? Check out this video!
